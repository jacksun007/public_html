Biography, Interests, and Hobbies


Kuei (Jack) Sun

ECE, University of Toronto


I studied at the University of Victoria for my bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with Software Specialization. The highlight of my study there was winning the artificial intelligence contest (for 3 more consecutive years as previous AI). I worked at Blue Castle Games (now Capcom Vancouver) as an Online Software Engineer for two years before deciding to start my journey to a Ph.D degree at the University of Toronto.


Interests and Hobbies

Web Design

I have acted as the web developer and system administrator for the OAPT contest website, which is an annual contest for Physics 11 students across Ontario. The framework was written in Python Django, running on Apache and nginx, using MySQL as our backend store. Unfortunately, graphics and animation is not one of my strong suits so please don't expect any awe-inspiring design.
