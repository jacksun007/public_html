Email: «kuei · sun @ mail · utoronto · ca»


I am a 3rd year Ph.D student at the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Toronto. My supervisors are Prof. Ashvin Goel and Prof. Angela Demke Brown. The focus of my research is on fully specifying the format of file systems, as well as examining storage applications that can benefit from a fully-specified file system.
