I am a 5th year Ph.D student at the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Toronto. My supervisors are Prof. Ashvin Goel and Prof. Angela Demke Brown. The focus of my research is on simplifying the development of file-system aware applications, as well as improving the robustness of these applications. The grand vision is to allow these applications to be written only once and be able to work across multiple file systems with little to no additional efforts required.
